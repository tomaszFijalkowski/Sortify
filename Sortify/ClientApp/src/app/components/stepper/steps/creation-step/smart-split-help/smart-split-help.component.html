Smart Split

Avoids splitting the playlist on either an album or an artist.

For example, if you were splitting a 200 track playlist into two 100 track ones and a 15 track album started at position 90, it would adjust the output to create 105 and 95 track playlists instead.

Use Smart Split if you want to contain the whole album (or artist respectively) in one playlist and don't mind adjusted playlist sizes.
